Golden Dragon's Games Recommendation

Top 10 Games I Played

#10- God of War

A thrilling adventure game with a rich story, immersive environment, and exciting monster-slaying gameplay. The relationship between Kratos and his son adds depth and emotion to the epic journey.


#9- TellTale The Walking Dead

A gripping narrative game with intense plot twists and emotional character arcs that keep you on the edge. SPOILER ALERT: almost every character will die, even the ones considered main characters.


#8- The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim

An expansive RPG with endless side quests and a vast world filled with adventures and mysteries to explore. Customize your character and experience the rich lore of the Elder Scrolls universe.


#7- Resident Evil Village

A horror survival game that combines chilling atmosphere, intense action, and intricate puzzles for a thrilling experience. Explore a gothic village filled with terrifying creatures and uncover its dark secrets.


#6- Life is Strange

A story-driven game with emotional depth, focusing on choices and consequences that impact the storyline. Follow Max as she discovers her time-rewinding powers and navigates complex relationships and mysteries.


#5- GTA San Andreas

An iconic open-world game with a compelling story, diverse missions, and a richly detailed world to explore. Join CJ on his journey to reclaim his family's control in the crime-ridden streets.


#4- COD Black Ops Series

A renowned FPS series known for its engaging campaign, addictive multiplayer, and beloved zombie mode. Experience intense combat, intriguing Cold War-era plots, and a variety of game modes that keep you hooked.


#3- Assassins Creed 2 (Trilogy)

A captivating series set in historical eras, featuring stealth gameplay, parkour, and a rich storyline. Follow Ezio Auditore's journey from novice to master assassin in beautifully recreated Renaissance Italy.


#2- Detroit: Become Human

A narrative-driven game with branching storylines based on player choices. Explore the lives of three androids in a futuristic Detroit, addressing themes of humanity, freedom, and the moral implications of AI.


#1- COD Modern Warfare

A groundbreaking FPS with a gripping campaign, realistic graphics, and intense multiplayer modes. Experience modern warfare scenarios with a focus on tactical gameplay and a story that explores the complexities of war.
